Activities

The Mayan communities of the Chuj ethnicity settled around the lake offer several activities that allow visitors to enjoy Laguna Brava's natural beauty.

The Lake Challenge

Put your strengths to work by crossing the four kilometers in a kayak gguided by a local guide. This adventure is physically demanding and recommended for only those in excellent physical condition.

  • Duration: 5 hours approximately.
  • Price: $50 USD
  • Min: 2 people
